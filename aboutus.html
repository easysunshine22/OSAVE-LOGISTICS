About Us

With our local service network in lagos, the OsAve Logistics Team is a reliable, freight forwarding, project logistics management Company. OsAve Logistics applies knowledge, business ethics with a strict compliance program, ingenuity, expertise and experience in managing today’s complex logistics challenges.

Our Goal

To provide stress free and affordable logistics services for export and import services to suite your business need.

Our Vision

To create a world where goods, raw material and finished products move quickly, and without borders from any location worldwide. To build a world where information technology and logistics shrink time and distance, ensuring competitive advantages for all our customers.

Our Values

Customers– We believe our clients are our best assets, and will go to any length possible to ensure complete satisfaction in all we do by creating and developing customized customer driven solutions at all times.
People– We will attract, develop and retain the best team members in an atmosphere that encourages open communication and respect for others, as well as constant training of team members.
Integrity– We will be honest, fair and reliable in our day-to-day interaction with customers and fellow employees.
Quality– We will accept nothing less than total and complete customer satisfaction, and will hold ourselves accountable for all our actions
